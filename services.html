Our Services

Over the years, Numelec has built a global reputation for delivering world-class healthcare services. From project consulting to after sales maintenance and support, Numelec goes the extra mile for ultimate client satisfaction. We provide qualitative rehabilitation of hospitals and also construct turnkey medical facilities. As exclusive representatives of leading manufacturers of a wide range of medical equipment and consumables, we distribute original and durable medical supplies.

We are also able to co-finance healthcare projects in collaboration with Development Finance Institutions (DFIs), Commercial Banks, National and Sub-National Governments and their Agencies. Given our pedigree, we are also adept at the provision of training for medical personnel, as this engenders effective technology transfer and human capital development.

We are proficient in the following areas:

  • Sale and Installation of Medical Equipment
  • Sale of Medical Consumables
  • After Sales Maintenance and Support
  • Project Management and Consultancy
  • Healthcare Project Financing
  • Hospital Rehabilitation and Construction
  • Training of Medical Personnel

About Numelec

With its International Headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland, Numelec has a global procurement and construction operations network spanning over 90 Countries across 5 Continents.
