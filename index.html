About Numelec Africa

Numelec Africa Holdings (NAH) is the Strategic Partner of the Numelec SA Group, Geneva, Switzerland, on the Continent of Africa. NAH warehouses the African operations of the Numelec SA Group, coordinating a Continent wide procurement and project management network spanning over Fifty (50) African countries.

Our Vision

To become the unrivalled international provider of biomedical, diagnostic and healthcare engineering solutions

Our Mission

To continuously provide innovative value-added healthcare solutions that engender good health and a qualitative life
